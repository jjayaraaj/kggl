Steel

Sodium silicate is used, along with magnesium silicate, in muffler repair and fitting paste.When dissolved in water, both sodium silicate and magnesium silicate form a thick paste that is easy to apply.

  • Sodium silicate is used as a binding agent in the production of refractory materials, which are essential for lining steelmaking furnaces and ladles.
  • It is added to cooling water systems in steel mills to help control the temperature and reduce the formation of slag, which can be detrimental to the steelmaking process.
  • It is utilized in wastewater treatment processes within steel mills to help in the coagulation and flocculation of impurities, making it easier to remove them from the water.
  • In the handling of steel materials, sodium silicate can be applied as a dust suppressant to minimize airborne particulate matter.
